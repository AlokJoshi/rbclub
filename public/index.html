Please note that this web-site is for current members of the club.All club members have been sent their login credentials: username and password. If you did not receive your credentials or if you have forgotten them you can gain access to the site by proving that you are a member of the club. You do this by

Providing your name and phone number which will both be checked against the club records.

If you get these right you will get a limited access to the site
